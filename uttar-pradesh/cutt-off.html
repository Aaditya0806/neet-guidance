Uttar Pradesh MBBS 2023 Cutoff

Types of  Uttar Pradesh  MBBS 2023 Cutoff

Uttar Pradesh MBBS cutoff  2023  for Government  and Private Medical  Colleges  can be classified in  following  Quotas:

For Government Medical Colleges : All Government Medical Colleges have  two types of Cutoffs

  1. 15% All India Quota Cutoff: The Directorate General of Health Services (DGHS) on behalf of MCC releases the  Uttarakhand All India Quota MBBS  cutoff  college-wise and category-wise  in online mode for the students selected under 15% All India Quota seats.
  1. 85% State Quota Cutoff: Directorate of Medical Education and Training (DMET), Lucknow will be regulatory body releases the Uttar Pradesh State Quota MBBS Cutoff college-wise, category-wise  in online mode for the students selected under 85% State Quota Seats.

For Private  Medical Colleges : All Private  Medical Colleges have One type of Cutoff

       1.  100% All India Management Quota Cutoff: Directorate of Medical Education and Training (DMET), Lucknow will be regulatory body releases the Uttar Pradesh MBBS Management Quota Cutoff college-wise, category-wise in online mode for the students selected under 100%  Management Quota Seats.

There are No NRI seats in U.P.
